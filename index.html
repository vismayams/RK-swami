OUR NETWORK


THE INDIAN NETWORK

The R K SWAMY BBDO | Hansa group has offices spanning seven cities - Bengaluru, Chennai, Delhi, Hyderabad, Kochi, Kolkata and Mumbai. Complementing this is a field network in 11 other cities. This allows us to deliver marketing communication solutions across the length and breadth of India. Hansa Marketing Services has US offices in Chicago, Illinois and Portland, Oregon.

THE GLOBAL NETWORK

BBDO Worldwide is the world’s most awarded advertising agency. With 15,000 employees in 289 offices across 81 countries, it is the second largest global advertising agency network, with headquarters in New York. The longevity of BBDO’s relationships with clients is built on trust, respect, and the impact of what is accomplished together. BBDO is wholly owned by the Omnicom Group, the world’s leading marketing communication group, listed on the NYSE. For more information visit www.bbdo.com
